Why MemoryGram?

It is where memos live forever. MemoryGram.com. It is an extraordinary place to meet people in private or with others and to celebrate their lives. The stories, pictures and videos we share help preserve our best memories. MemoryGram.com honours the privacy of your memories. For any memorial you make, you have full control over the privacy settings. Share some pictures and tales, while others are only available for viewing. The access to your whole memory vault can also be regulated.

Come together with friends, family and loved ones for online story-sharing. View and add to established memorials, or create your own and invite others to join you.
